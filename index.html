Bio / Chris Carruthers

Cross-Discipline Digital Consultancy Design with purpose

Full Stack / CMS / Interface

I'm a self-taught UI Designer & Full Stack Developer, delivering intuitive web experiences for corporate and b2b clients and consultation for digital agencies.

I collaborate with start-ups, NPOs, SMEs and creatives in various capacities, crafting full service digital solutions for brands such as YMCA, British Telecom, Ciaté, Canterbury, Toni & Guy and AEL.

I'm Founder of Cult Digital and The Molyneux Network.

To
Draw Inspiration

I'm lucky enough to work with a number of talented B2B clients helping to deliver front-end development, database driven CMS and e-Commerce platforms. I regularly build custom WordPress themes, responsive web interfaces and email marketing campaigns.

Collaborative working processes often form organically and range from a few notes to full scale proposals, with wireframing, support documentation and prototyping.

An effective response strategy should include key web technologies, UX trends, standards compliance and cross-compatability. Additional considerations are client analysis, scheduling & budget outlines. Testing is a crucial part of the process and includes usability, security and debugging. Finally, deployment is unique for each project and can take various forms from a multi-media campaign launch to a 'soft' test and review launch. Common considerations for deployment are analytics & feedback, usability and SEO.
